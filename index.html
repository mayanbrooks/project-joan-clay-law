OUR PHILOSOPHY

The worlds of real estate and business are constantly changing. Individuals and businesses need a legal representative who understands the law as it affects their interests.

They also need someone who is ethical, accessible and hardworking. I have spent my career not only as an attorney, but as a trial technology consultant and legal marketer, so I understand the business side of the law.

I am dedicated to practicing limited areas of law enabling me to offer my clients a wealth of experience with a proven track record in the complex needs in the areas of real estate and business law. I serve as an advisor and advocate, and provide the highest quality legal work.
